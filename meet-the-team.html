Meet the Team

The following information will tell you about the team, previous years of expirence, and their role.

Driver: Anjalika 

Pit crew: Before Mrigaj started FTC, he did FLL (Lego League). His job on the team is to handle nearly all the hardware related items and to ensure the robot is working up to the high expectations before any match including practice ones. Mrigaj is on pit crew this means that after a match there could be a hidden issue in the robot that could be crucial if not fixed or discovered by the pit crew. Mrigaj's rookie year was 2024 were he started to learn CAD and the pit crew. Mirgaj has learned many items that help him do his role on the team more efficiently. 

Drive coach, programmer: Tirth is the drive coach for our team! He helps the drivers on the field and gives them advice on how to improve in future matches. The 2024 season was Tirths rookie year in FTC robotics, his job on the team then was pit crew. Since it was his first year, he knew very little about robotics but drastically improving over the year has helped him use his knowledge to be a drive coach now. 

Human Player, PR: Anna's main job on the team is to design all the graphics for the team's PR and function of as the Human Player at competitions. She is the PR lead which means she controls our main social media platforms and bring encouraging STEM messages to the community. Annas rookie year was 2024 where she was also PR lead.

Team Lead, Designer, Programmer: Adrian is the team captain, this means he ensures everyone on the team has something to do that they enjoy and see the benefits of each decision the team makes. He also over sees our CAD and design process to make sure that everything will run smoothly or efficiently both at competitions and at the build space. 

Pit Crew: Carter is the lead for the pit crew this means his role on the team is to make sure robot leaves the robot in perfect condition for every match 

Programmer: Ilyas's job on the team is to write, test, and improve the code that controls the robot’s actions and movements. His job also requires him to check with the rest of the team on how smooth the code is running. The 2025 season is Ilyas's rookie year, even though this is his first year he has obtained lots of knowledge about coding. 

Operator, Programmer: Mahie's job on our team is to be the operator this means she controls the robot's arm and claw mechanisms. During competitions she has to carefully collaborate with the driver so they can efficiently work.
