About

Hyperdroid is an FTC team who team strive for success. This can be proven by looking at previous years of success where they have gone to the Alberta Championships every season for the last three years and have qualified for World Championships 2025 and 2024.

How to support out team 

FIRST robotics relys on volunteers this includes mentors. We need help to cover costs and here are some ways you can support us:

Corporate Sponsorship - reach out to info@ataarobotics.ca and we will work with you to promote your organization while you support ours.

Personal Donation - we always accept e-transfered funds directly to payment@ataarobotics.ca
