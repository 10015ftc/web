10015 Hyperdroid: FTC robotics team 

Part of ATAA, a non-profit based in Calgary, Alberta that suports STEM education through FIRST robotics programs. 

Hyperdroid is a FTC team, this team strives for success. This can be proven by looking at previous years of success where they have gone to the Alberta Championships twice and have gone to the World Championships once.

Over the past season this team has won many awards such as:

Design award: This award is given to the team who has the most efficent and eye catching design. The team has to keep in mind aesthetics and function while making the design.

Innovate award Finalists: The innovate award is given to the team that has solutions for their robot on the spotthis would also include high pressure situations such as competitions. This, "[a]ward celebrates a team that thinks imaginatively and has the ingenuity, creativity, and inventiveness".

Inspire award 3rd place: The inspire award is given to the team who helps out their community for the better in any way possible by using Gracious Professionalism on and off the field. The team would share the knowlege the obtained during the season to help other so that they can have the same chances.

Outreach:This term is used to define someone promoting STEM and FIRST through community events and mentoring. Outreach is a key item in robotics because you can help other teams succeed so they have a fun season. Some outreach our team has done during the 2025 season is:

Working closely and providing advice to: 4 Aberhart High School robotics teams (22212, 13898, 11712, 20176) and STEM Inovation Academy teams (27272, 27953, 27273).

One item out team has accomplished and helped the community is by fixing toys for the Renfrew Educational School. This school is for special needs children which means the toys that have need to be adapted so every kid can use them. This process is very expenisive so our organization (including our team) has helped in fixing these toys for free.  The toys we fixed were toys that were already owned by the school.
