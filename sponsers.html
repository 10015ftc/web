Sponsers 

 

Sponsers play a vital role in the success of FIRST teams. Without their support teams would face grand challenges in covering expenses asosicated with building their robot and obtaining necessary materials and tools. Sponsers donate money to teams so they can focus on building an efficiant robot and teamwork rather than being burdened by limited resources. Without sponsors, FIRST teams would not be able to function effectively, and the opportunities for growth, learning, and competition would be severely limited.
